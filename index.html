Nice to meet you!

Hi! I’m Bailey Roggen — a passionate and curious multimedia web development student with a love for creating meaningful digital experiences.

I have recently finished my Associates of Applied Science in Multimedia Web Development at Skagit Valley College (Whidbey Campus).

I really enjoy working on projects where creativity and problem-solving come together. Whether I’m collaborating with a team or alone, I’m always open to feedback and love using it to grow. I also have experience communicating with clients and teammates, and I’m excited to bring that energy into a real-world web development role.

Right now, I’m looking for an entry-level position in web development where I can continue to grow professionally and apply the skills I’ve built through school and personal projects. I’m especially interested in front-end development, UI/UX design, and projects that bring together function and aesthetics. I have hands-on experience with Adobe XD for app and interface design, and I’ve created fully functional websites using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. I’ve also begun working with MySQL for basic database integration. I’m eager to join a collaborative team where I can contribute to building user-friendly, visually engaging, and accessible digital experiences.
