Nice to meet you!

Hi! I’m Bailey Roggen — a passionate and curious multimedia web development student with a love for creating meaningful digital experiences.

I'm currently working toward my Associate of Applied Science in Multimedia Web Development at Skagit Valley College (Whidbey Campus), and I’m set to graduate in 2025. Before that, I spent a year at Cerro Coso Community College in California, where I built a solid foundation in digital design and front-end development.

I really enjoy working on projects where creativity and problem-solving come together. Whether I’m collaborating with a team or alone, I’m always open to feedback and love using it to grow. I also have experience communicating with clients and teammates, and I’m excited to bring that energy into a real-world web development role.

Right now, I’m looking for an entry-level position where I can keep learning, level up my skills, and be part of building user-friendly websites.
